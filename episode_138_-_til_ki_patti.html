Transparent Sesame Patties

What makes this dish special

These transparent sesame patties are a unique and delicious treat that will impress your friends and family. Made with just two ingredients, sesame seeds and sugar, these patties are surprisingly easy to make. The key to their transparency is to roll them out very thinly while the caramel is still hot. The result is a delicate, crispy, and sweet treat that is perfect for snacking or as a garnish for desserts. The patties are so thin that they almost melt in your mouth, and the sesame seeds add a nutty flavor that is simply irresistible.

Recipe

Ingredients: * Sugar (2-3 Tbsp) * White Sesame Seeds (2-3 Tbsp) * Parchment Paper

Instructions:

  1. Caramelize the sugar in a small, flat-bottomed pan over medium heat.
  2. On a piece of parchment paper, pour a tablespoon of the hot caramelized sugar.
  3. Top with a tablespoon of roasted sesame seeds.
  4. Quickly cover with another piece of parchment paper.
  5. Roll out the mixture very thinly using a rolling pin.
  6. Once cooled, gently remove the patty from the parchment paper using a butter knife.
  7. Store the patties lined with parchment paper in an airtight container.

Cooking Tips: * It is better to make the caramelized sugar in small batches since it needs to be hot. * If you caramelize the sugar too darkly, the patties will taste bitter.

Architecture Tip

When building a house in places where the water table is very high, you will encounter water when digging the foundation. A simple solution is to dig a deeper pit next to the foundation. The water pressure will cause the water to go into the deeper pit, allowing the foundation to dry.
