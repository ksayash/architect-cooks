Roasted Cashew Whole Wheat Cookies

What makes this dish special

These cookies are a quick and easy treat that can be made with ingredients you likely already have in your pantry. The recipe uses whole wheat flour, making them a healthier alternative to traditional cookies. The roasted cashews add a nutty flavor and satisfying crunch. Ghee is used instead of butter, giving the cookies a rich and slightly different flavor profile. These cookies are perfect for a quick snack or a sweet treat with a cup of coffee or tea. They are also a great option for those looking for a healthier cookie recipe.

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup (63g) Whole Wheat flour
  • 2 Tbsp (28g) Ghee
  • 3 Tbsp (33g) Caster sugar
  • ¼ tsp Baking powder
  • 2 tsp Roasted cashew (broken)
  • ⅛ tsp Salt
  • 1 ½ - 2 Tbsp Milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 170 degrees Celsius.
  2. In a mixing bowl, add the ghee and caster sugar and whisk for a minute or two, till fluffy.
  3. Add the flour, salt, and cashew and mix well with a fork.
  4. Knead gently with hand to bring the dough together. Add milk as required.
  5. Shape the dough into cookies and make a pattern with a fork.
  6. Bake for 15-18 minutes till golden brown.
  7. Let the cookies cool for 15-20 minutes before serving.
