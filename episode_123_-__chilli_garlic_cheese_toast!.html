Chili Cheese Garlic Toast

What makes this dish special

This is a quick and easy snack that is sure to be a hit with both kids and adults. It's a versatile dish that can be enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. The combination of chili flakes, garlic, and herbs creates a flavorful and aromatic experience. The cheese adds a creamy and rich element that complements the other ingredients perfectly. This toast is a great way to use up leftover bread and cheese, and it can be customized to your liking by adding different toppings or spices. The recipe is simple enough for even novice cooks to follow, and it can be made in just a few minutes.

Recipe

Ingredients: * Bread slices: 2 (white or brown) * Butter: 2 tsp (soft) * Garlic: ½ tsp (minced) * Red chili flakes: 1 tsp * Mixed herbs: 1 tsp * Cheese: 2 cubes (grated) * Coriander: for garnishing (finely chopped)

Instructions: 1. Preheat the OTG on bake mode at 180 degrees Celsius. 2. Mix red chili flakes, garlic, and herbs in the butter. 3. Apply liberally on one side of the bread slices. 4. Sprinkle grated cheese. 5. Garnish with coriander, red chili flakes, and herbs. 6. Bake until the cheese is light golden and the bread is crisp. 7. Serve hot with a cup of coffee, tea, or a cold drink.
