Baby Potato and Spinach Salad

What makes this dish special

This dish is a salad, but it can be a full-fledged meal in itself. It is a very tasty dish that is quick to make. It can be served with garlic toast or butter toast, along with a cup of hot soup. The baby potatoes are boiled with the skin on, which gives it a slight crunch. The spinach is blanched to retain the color and nutrients. The mayonnaise dressing is simple, but flavorful.

Recipe

Ingredients: * Baby potatoes: 250g (washed well and boiled with skin on) * Spinach: 250g (washed well and chopped finely) * Mayonnaise: 2-3 Tbsp * Salt: to taste * Black pepper powder: to taste * Lemon juice: 1 Tbsp * Garlic: ½ tsp (minced)

Instructions:

  1. Blanche the spinach for 30 seconds, drain, and squeeze out all the water.
  2. To the mayonnaise, add the salt, pepper, lemon juice, and garlic. Mix well.
  3. In case the potatoes are big, cut into halves or else leave whole.
  4. Mix the spinach with the mayonnaise.
  5. Now add the potatoes and mix well.
  6. Serve chilled with a side of garlic/butter toast and soup or as side with any main course.

Cooking tip: Blanching the spinach helps retain the color and nutrients.
