Pakodiyan/Boondi Sabzi

What makes this dish special

This dish is a childhood favorite of the creator and her sisters. It's a simple recipe with ingredients that are normally found in most homes. It's a great dish to make when you don't know what to cook. It's a quick and easy dish that can be made in about 7-8 minutes. The dish is made with pakodiyan, which is known as boondi in other parts of India. The dish is a mix of colors from the turmeric, chili, and coriander. It's a dish that is sure to be a hit with the whole family.

Recipe

Ingredients: * Pakodiyan/Boondi: 1 heaping cup * Chopped onion: 2 medium * Chopped tomato: 1 * Water: 2-3 Tbsp * Oil * Salt to taste * Red chili powder: 1 tsp * Coriander powder: 1 tsp * Chopped coriander: 1 Tbsp * Chopped green chilies * Turmeric: 1/2 tsp

Instructions: 1. In a kadai/pan, add the oil. Once hot, add the onion. Cook until it just starts to change color. 2. Add the tomatoes. Cook until the tomatoes are slightly soft (not mushy). 3. Add green chilies and all spices. Mix well. 4. Add the Pakodiyan. Toss once. 5. Add half the coriander. Now sprinkle the water, put off the flame, mix once and put on a lid. 6. Rest for 5 to 10 minutes. 7. After 10 minutes, take lid off, mix once. Serve, garnished with rest of the coriander leaves.

Cooking tip: Remember that the pakodiyan are salted, so you only need to add salt for the mixture.
