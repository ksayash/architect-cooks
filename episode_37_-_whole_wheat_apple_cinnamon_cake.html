Apple Cinnamon Whole Wheat Cake

What makes this dish special

This is a healthy cake recipe that uses whole wheat flour. It is an apple cinnamon cake that is very tasty. The apples are peeled and grated, and then mixed with lemon juice and cinnamon powder. The cake is topped with thinly sliced apples that are coated with lemon juice and cinnamon powder. The cinnamon gives the cake a very nice flavor. The cake is baked in the oven and then cooled on a wire rack. It is best served warm with a cup of coffee.

Recipe

Ingredients: * 1 cup whole wheat flour * 1 tsp baking powder * 1 tsp baking soda * ¾ cup sugar * ¼ cup walnuts, chopped * ¼ cup oil * ½ cup milk, plus 2 Tbsp if required * 1 tsp cinnamon powder * 1 cup grated peeled apple, mixed with some cinnamon and lemon juice * 2 medium apples, cut into semi circles and coated with cinnamon and lemon juice * 1 tsp vanilla essence * Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius. Grease and line a baking dish.
  2. Mix the sugar with all the wet ingredients along with the grated apples.
  3. Mix together all the dry ingredients.
  4. Mix the wet and dry ingredients.
  5. Pour into the baking dish and tap.
  6. Top with the sliced apples as shown.
  7. Bake for 40 to 55 minutes till done.
  8. Take out and cool on a wire rack for at least 15-20 minutes.
  9. Serve hot.

Cooking Tips: * Grate the apples a little early so they get a little mushy. * Do not cut the cake while it is warm, as it will crumble. * If you want to serve the cake warm, microwave it for 30 seconds.

Architecture Tip

The tip is about the disposal of kitchen waste. The kitchen waste that is green, like peels of vegetables or egg shells, should not be put in the normal waste. If you live in a house with a front or back yard, dig a 2 ft x 2 ft x 2 ft hole in the soil and put your kitchen waste in that. Also add leaves, twigs, or grass that is cut in the garden. Within 2-3 months, you will have a great fertilizer to put in the plants.
