Roasted Peanut Chaat

What makes this dish special

This roasted peanut chaat is a quick and easy snack that is both healthy and delicious. It's a great option for an evening snack or appetizer, and it's sure to be a hit with people of all ages. The roasted peanuts provide a satisfying crunch, while the fresh vegetables add a burst of flavor and nutrients. The combination of spices and lemon juice creates a tangy and savory taste that is simply irresistible. This chaat is also very versatile, as you can easily customize it to your liking by adding or substituting ingredients. For example, you can add some chopped mango or pomegranate seeds for a touch of sweetness, or you can use different types of sev for a unique texture.

Recipe

Ingredients: * 1 cup raw peanuts (roasted) * 2 Tbsp onion (finely chopped) * 2 Tbsp tomatoes (finely chopped) * 2 Tbsp cucumber (finely chopped) * 2 Tbsp capsicum (finely chopped) * 2 green chilies (finely chopped) * 2 Tbsp coriander (finely chopped) * Salt to taste * ½ tsp black salt * ½ tsp roasted jeera powder * ½ tsp chaat masala * ½ tsp Kashmiri red chili powder * 2 Tbsp lemon juice * Sev for topping * Papad cones for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix everything well.
  2. Serve chilled, garnished with coriander and sev.
  3. It can be served in papad cones also for a twist.
