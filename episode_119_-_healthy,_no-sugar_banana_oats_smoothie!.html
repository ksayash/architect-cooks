Banana Oats Smoothie

What makes this dish special

This banana oats smoothie is a healthy and tasty drink that can be enjoyed as a breakfast dish or an evening snack. It's a full meal in itself and doesn't require any added sugar. The combination of banana, oats, and dates provides natural sweetness and essential nutrients. The addition of flax seeds and almonds further enhances the nutritional value and adds a delightful crunch. This smoothie is a quick and easy way to incorporate healthy ingredients into your diet. It's perfect for those looking for a nutritious and satisfying option without compromising on taste. The smoothie is a great way to start the day or refuel after a workout.

Recipe

Ingredients: * Rolled oats: 4 Tbsp * Dates: 4-5 (seedless) * Banana: 1 medium (chopped) * Milk: 1 cup

For garnishing: * Flax seeds: 1 tsp * Almonds: 5-6 (sliced)

Instructions: 1. Soak the dates and oats in milk for 10 minutes. 2. Add the soaked mixture and banana to a blender. 3. Blend until smooth. 4. Pour into a glass. 5. Garnish with flax seeds and sliced almonds. 6. Serve chilled.
