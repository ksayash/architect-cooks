Instant Whole Wheat Bread

What makes this dish special

This recipe is for a quick and easy whole wheat bread that is perfect for any occasion. This bread is made without eggs or yeast, making it a great option for those with dietary restrictions. It is also a healthy alternative to store-bought bread, as it is made with whole wheat flour and honey. The bread is soft and moist, with a slightly sweet flavor. The sesame seeds add a nice crunch and nutty flavor. This bread is perfect for toast, sandwiches, or just a simple snack. It is also a great way to use up any leftover milk you may have.

Recipe

Ingredients: * Whole wheat: 3 cups * Milk: 2 cups (lukewarm) * Vinegar: 2 Tbsp * Honey: 1/4 cup * Salt: 1/8 tsp * Baking soda: 2 tsp * Oil: 2 Tbsp * Sesame seeds: 1 Tbsp * Butter: 1 tsp

Instructions: 1. Pre heat the oven on bake mode to 150 degrees Celsius. 2. Mix together the milk and vinegar and keep aside. 3. In a big bowl, add the wheat flour with the baking soda and salt. Mix well. 4. After 5 minutes, the milk - vinegar mixture will curdle slightly. To this add the honey and oil. Mix well. 5. Grease a baking dish. 6. Add the wet ingredients to the dry and mix well to form a homogeneous mixture. Do not over mix. 7. Pour the batter into the baking dish. 8. Sprinkle with Sesame seeds. 9. Apply a wash of milk. 10. Bake for 30 minutes, cover top with foil, bake for another 25 minutes. 11. Take out and apply some butter on top. 12. Cover with a cloth and let it cool on a wire rack. Cut only after a couple of hours.
