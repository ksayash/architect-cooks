Hyderabadi Baingan ka Salan

What makes this dish special

Baingan ka Salan is a famous Hyderabadi dish that is both flavorful and easy to make. This recipe uses long brinjals, which are slit and fried. Potatoes are added to the dish to add a different texture and flavor. The dish is made with a variety of spices, including peanuts, sesame seeds, coconut, and red chilies. Tamarind pulp is added to give the dish a tangy flavor. The dish is garnished with green chilies and chopped coriander. This dish is best served with hot parathe or steamed rice.

Recipe

Ingredients: * Long brinjals: 500g (slit and fried) * Potatoes: 2 medium (cut into wedges and fried) * Green chillies: 5-6 (slit and fried) * Tamarind pulp: ½ cup * Peanuts: 2 Tbsp (roasted) * Sesame seeds: 2 Tbsp (roasted) * Coconut (desiccated or fresh grated): 2 Tbsp (roasted) * Dry red chillies: 4-5 * Curry leaves: 10-12 * Mustard seeds: ½ tsp * Kalonji (Nigella): ½ tsp * Onion paste: 1 medium onion * Ginger paste: ½ tsp * Garlic paste: 1 tsp * Mustard oil: 2 Tbsp (can use plain veg oil too) * Salt: to taste * Red chilli powder: to taste * Haldi: ½ tsp * Coriander powder: 1 tsp * Cumin powder: ½ tsp * Coriander: 1 tsp (chopped)

Instructions:

  1. Grind peanuts, sesame, dry red chillies, and coconut into a fine paste.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add the mustard, Kalonji, and curry leaves.
  3. Once they splutter, add the onion paste.
  4. Saute till pink and add the ginger-garlic paste.
  5. Saute till raw smell goes.
  6. Add all dry spices and cook with a Tbsp of water.
  7. Now add the blended mixture.
  8. Cook till oil leaves the sides.
  9. Add a cup of water and once it comes to a boil, add the brinjal and potatoes.
  10. Let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  11. Now add the tamarind pulp and bring to a boil.
  12. Adjust consistency if required.
  13. Just before serving, add the green chillies and garnish with chopped coriander.

Architecture Tip

It is very important to use top-quality pipes and fixtures for the hidden pipes in the toilet. If you use poor-quality pipes, they may leak, and it will be very difficult to repair them. You will have to break down the tiles, break the wall, and break the floor to repair the pipes. This will save a lot of effort at a later stage.
