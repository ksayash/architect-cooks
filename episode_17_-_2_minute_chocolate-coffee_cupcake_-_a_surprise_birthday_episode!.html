Coffee Walnut Cupcake

What makes this dish special

This coffee walnut cupcake is a quick and easy treat, perfect for a single serving dessert. The combination of cocoa powder and coffee powder creates a rich, mocha-like flavor that is enhanced by the addition of chopped walnuts. The recipe is designed to be simple and foolproof, even for novice cooks. It's a great way to satisfy a sweet craving without the fuss of traditional baking. The cupcake is cooked in a microwave, making it a convenient option for a quick dessert. The addition of fresh cream and curd adds moisture and richness to the cake, resulting in a soft and spongy texture.

Recipe

Ingredients: * 6 Tbsp all-purpose flour (maida) * 2 Tbsp cocoa powder * 3 Tbsp castor sugar * ½ tsp coffee powder * ½ tsp baking powder * Pinch of baking soda * Pinch of salt * 2 Tbsp chopped walnuts * 4 Tbsp milk * 2 Tbsp fresh cream * 2 Tbsp curd * 2 Tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. In a suitable-sized cup or coffee mug, combine all the dry ingredients (flour, cocoa powder, sugar, coffee powder, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and walnuts). Mix well with a fork.
  2. Add all the wet ingredients (milk, fresh cream, curd, and oil) to the cup. Mix well, stirring in one direction only.
  3. Microwave on full power for 2 minutes.
  4. Leave the cupcake inside the microwave for at least 5 more minutes to rest.
  5. Take out and serve hot, either plain or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Architecture Tip

Paints have advanced a lot. You may remember enamel paint, which was used on doors and windows. The main purpose was that it could be wiped clean and didn't stain easily. However, it had a glossy finish and these are the days of matte finishes. All the paint companies have now developed enamel paints in a matte finish. They have the qualities of a good enamel paint, that they are easy to wipe clean, but they are in a matte finish so they go well with your decor.
