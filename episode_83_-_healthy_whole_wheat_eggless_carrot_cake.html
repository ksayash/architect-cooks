Healthy Carrot Cake with Mixed Nuts

What makes this dish special

This recipe is a healthy take on a classic carrot cake. Instead of using all-purpose flour, this recipe uses whole wheat flour, making it a more nutritious option. It also uses jaggery instead of sugar, adding a unique flavor and sweetness. The addition of mixed nuts provides a delightful crunch and nutty flavor. The cake is moist and flavorful, thanks to the combination of grated carrots, curd, and fresh cream. It's a perfect treat for those looking for a guilt-free indulgence. The recipe is simple to follow and can be made in a jiffy.

Recipe

Ingredients: * Whole Wheat flour: 3/4 cup + 1 tbsp * Carrot: 1/2 cup (grated) * Curd: 1/2 cup (beaten) * Vegetable Oil: 1/4 cup * Jaggery: 1/2 cup (ground coarsely) * Baking powder: 1/2 tsp * Baking soda: 1/2 tsp * Salt: 1/2 tsp * Fresh Cream: 1 tbsp * Cinnamon powder: 1 tsp * Vanilla essence: 1/2 tsp * Mixed nuts: 1 cup (almonds, walnuts, cashews) * Milk: if required

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven on bake mode to 160 degrees Celsius.
  2. Grease a baking dish of appropriate size.
  3. In a big bowl, mix together jaggery, oil, curd, cream, and vanilla essence.
  4. In another bowl, mix all dry ingredients. Now add the carrot and half the nuts. Mix well.
  5. Mix the dry ingredients into the wet bowl. Mix everything well. If required add a spoon or 2 of milk to get the required consistency.
  6. Pour into the baking dish. Garnish with the remaining nuts.
  7. Bake for 25 to 35 minutes.
  8. Take out, once done and put it on a wire rack to cool.
  9. After 10 to 15 minutes, serve with tea/coffee, for a healthy snack.
