Braised Potatoes with Soy Sauce and Honey

What makes this dish special

Braised potatoes with soy sauce and honey are a quick and easy side dish that is both delicious and visually appealing. The baby potatoes are cooked in a sauce made from soy sauce and honey, which gives them a sweet and savory flavor. The dish is garnished with sesame seeds and coriander, which add a touch of elegance. The potatoes are first browned in oil to give them a crispy exterior, then braised in the sauce until they are tender. This dish is perfect for a quick weeknight meal or a special occasion. It is also a great way to use up leftover potatoes. The combination of flavors is sure to please everyone at the table.

Recipe

Ingredients: * Baby potatoes: 500g (washed with skin on) * Oil: 2 Tbsp * Water: 1 cup * Soy sauce: 2 ½ Tbsp (dark) or 5 Tbsp (light) * Honey: 3 Tbsp * Salt: to taste * Red chili flakes: 1 tsp * Garlic: 1 tsp (finely chopped) * Sesame seeds: 1Tbsp + ½ Tbsp * Coriander: 1 Tbsp (finely chopped)

Instructions: 1. Cut the potatoes into halves. 2. In a heavy-bottomed pan, heat the oil and add the potatoes. 3. Cook on high heat for 7-10 minutes, tossing at intervals, until the edges are golden brown. 4. Add the garlic, chili flakes, and salt. Toss well. 5. Mix the soy sauce and honey with the water. Add the mix to the potatoes. 6. Turn the heat to medium and cook with the lid on until the water turns into a saucy consistency. Check to see that the potatoes are tender, else add a little more water. 7. Once done, add 1Tbsp of the sesame seeds. Mix well. 8. Serve hot, garnished with sesame and coriander.
