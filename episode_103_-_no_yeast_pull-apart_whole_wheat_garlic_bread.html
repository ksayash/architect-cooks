Whole Wheat Pull Apart Bread

What makes this dish special

This is a delicious bread recipe that is made with whole wheat flour and is packed with flavor. The bread is a pull-apart bread, so you don't need to slice it. You can simply pull apart the slices with your hands. The bread is made with a variety of flavors, including garlic, onion, red chili flakes, and mixed herbs. It is also made without yeast, so it is quick and easy to make. The bread has a wonderful aroma when it is baking, and it is sure to be a hit with everyone who tries it.

Recipe

Ingredients: * 1 1/2 cups + 1 Tbsp whole wheat flour * 3/4 cup lukewarm milk * 1 Tbsp white vinegar * 2 Tbsp oil with roasted garlic * 2 Tbsp honey * 1 Tbsp sesame seeds * 1 Tbsp chopped and fried garlic * 1 Tbsp chopped and fried onion * 1 tsp red chili flakes * 1 tsp mixed herbs * Salt to taste * 1 tsp baking soda

For lining between the slices: * 2 Tbsp melted butter * 1 Tbsp chopped coriander * 1/2 tsp red chili flakes * 1/2 tsp oregano

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 150 degrees Celsius on bake mode.
  2. Add the vinegar to warm milk and put aside.
  3. Mix together all dry ingredients except sesame seeds.
  4. After a few minutes (once milk curdles) add the honey and oil.
  5. Add wet ingredients to dry. Mix first with a fork and then by hand to form a dough.
  6. Divide into 16 balls.
  7. Roll and line with butter mixture and place on top of the other.
  8. Put 4 together, cut into semi circles and place into a greased baking dish.
  9. Work with all the pieces similarly as shown in the video.
  10. Top with the sesame seeds and chopped coriander and wash with milk.
  11. Put in the oven for 55 minutes.
  12. After 30 minutes cover lightly with a piece of foil.
  13. Brush hot bread with butter mixture and cover with a cloth.
  14. Put on a rack to cool. Use after at least 3 to 4 hours.

Architecture Tip

If you are building a new house and you want to paint the walls, normally two to three coats are given over the primer. The tip is that after the primer is applied and the first coat of paint is applied, don't immediately apply the second coat. Only after the finishing, after your fans have been put up, after your light fixtures have been put up, so that any smudges that might occur on the walls can be taken care of, should you apply the second and final coat of paint.
