Paneer Butter Masala

What makes this dish special

Paneer butter masala is a popular and tasty dish that is quick and easy to make. It is a rich dish with a creamy gravy. The red color of the gravy comes from ripe red tomatoes and Kashmiri red chili powder. The sweetness of the dish comes from tomato ketchup. The dish is garnished with green coriander and a few drops of fresh cream. The recipe is made in two steps. The first step is to cook the tomato and onion gravy. The second step is to blend the gravy and add the paneer.

Recipe

Ingredients: * Paneer: 250g (cut into cubes) * Onion: 1 large (roughly chopped) * Tomatoes: 3 large (roughly chopped) * Mace/Javitri: 1 small piece * Green cardamom: 4-5 * Cloves: 4-5 * Peppercorns: 4-5 * Cashews: 12-15 * Tomato ketchup: 1 Tbsp * Fresh cream/malai: 2 Tbsp * Kasuri methi: 1 Tbsp (roasted and crushed) * Ginger paste: ½ tsp * Garlic paste: 1 tsp * Salt: to taste * Kashmiri red chili powder: 1 Tbsp * Garam masala powder: ½ tsp * Butter: 2 Tbsp * Oil: 2 Tbsp * Green Coriander: 1 tsp (finely chopped)

Instructions: 1. In a kadai, take 1 Tbsp oil and 1 Tbsp of butter. Add the dry whole spices. Sauté for a minute. Add the onions. Sauté till translucent. Add the ginger - garlic paste and cashews. Mix well. Add the salt and half the red chili powder. Mix well. Add the tomatoes. Cook till soft. Add half a cup of water, bring to a boil and with lid on, let it simmer till the water evaporates. 2. Once cool, blend the mixture finely. 3. In the same kadai, add rest of the oil and butter. Once hot, add the rest of the red chilli powder and then quickly add the blended mixture. Sauté for 3 to 4 minutes. Add the fresh cream, kasuri methi and garam masala. Mix Well. Add some water to adjust the consistency. Add the paneer cubes and sauté for 2 to 3 minutes. Serve hot, garnished with green coriander and a few drops of fresh cream. 4. If you are not serving immediately, add the paneer just before serving to prevent it from getting hard.
