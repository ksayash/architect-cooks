Strawberry Whole Wheat Eggless Cake

What makes this dish special

This strawberry whole wheat eggless cake is a healthy and delicious treat that is perfect for any occasion. It is made with whole wheat flour, which is a good source of fiber, and it is eggless, making it a great option for those with egg allergies. The cake is also packed with fresh strawberries, which add a natural sweetness and flavor. This cake is a great way to enjoy a healthy and delicious dessert without any artificial colors or essences. It is moist, flavorful, and perfect for a summer gathering. The fresh strawberries make it a beautiful and festive dessert.

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup + 1 Tbsp whole wheat flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • A pinch of salt
  • ¾ cup strawberry puree
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup refined oil
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • ½ tsp vanilla essence
  • 2 Tbsp chopped strawberries
  • Strawberry slices for decoration
  • Caster sugar for garnishing

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius on bake mode.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, put milk, sugar, oil, vanilla essence, strawberry puree, and lemon juice. Mix everything well.
  3. Mix flour with salt, baking powder, and baking soda. Add the chopped strawberries and mix.
  4. Add the dry ingredients into the wet mixture. Gently fold. Adjust the consistency by adding milk if required.
  5. Garnish with sliced strawberries.
  6. Bake it for 35-40 minutes until done.
  7. Once cool, demold it, slice it, dust some caster sugar, if desired, and enjoy this delicious moist cake.

Cooking tip: Take care that the oil is flavorless and odorless.

Architecture Tip

When building a new house or apartment, it may seem lucrative to use single-phase wiring and a single-phase meter to save costs in the beginning. However, this is a mistake because as the years pass by, the number of electrical gadgets in the house will only increase. If you have to change the wiring and meter later, it becomes a major headache. Therefore, it is better to spend a little extra in the beginning and provide for three-phase wiring and a three-phase meter.
