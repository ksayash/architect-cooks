Quick Aata

What makes this dish special

This recipe isn't a dish, but rather a method for preparing the dough for roti or paratha. It's a quick and easy way to prepare the dough, perfect for busy weeknights. The method involves mixing the flour and water with a spoon, letting it rest for 10 minutes, and then kneading it for just a minute. This results in a soft and pliable dough that's easy to work with. This method is especially useful when you don't have a lot of time to spend in the kitchen. It's also great for beginners who are just learning how to make roti or paratha.

Recipe

Ingredients: * 1 heaping cup whole wheat flour * 1/2 cup water * Extra water in a bowl

Instructions: 1. In a mixing bowl, take the wheat flour and add half cup water. 2. Mix well with a spoon and cover with a lid. 3. Keep aside for 10 minutes. 4. Wet your hand with some water and mix the flour for about 1 minute. You are done. 5. Take out the kneaded flour in a small container and pat it with some water. 6. Put on a tight lid and store in the fridge.
