Ginger and Garlic Paste

What makes this dish special

Ginger and garlic are essential ingredients in Indian cooking. They are used in almost every recipe. However, preparing them can be time-consuming. This recipe provides a way to prepare ginger and garlic paste in advance and store them for months. This makes day-to-day cooking much easier. The recipe is simple and easy to follow. The paste can be stored in the fridge for up to a month or in the freezer for up to six months. This recipe is a great way to save time and effort in the kitchen.

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 100g ginger, peeled and chopped
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, grind the ginger and salt without water.
  2. Stir in a clean airtight container.
  3. Store in the fridge for up to a month or in the freezer for up to six months.

Ingredients:

  • 100g garlic, peeled
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, grind the garlic and salt without water.
  2. Stir in a clean airtight container.
  3. Store in the fridge for up to a month or in the freezer for up to six months.

Tips:

  • The blending jar should be completely dry.
  • Do not add any water to the blender.
  • Use a spatula to scrape the sides of the blender while grinding.
  • Salt helps to grind the ginger and garlic more easily.
  • Salt acts as a preservative.
