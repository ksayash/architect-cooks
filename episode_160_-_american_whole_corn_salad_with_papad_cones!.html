American Whole Corn Salad

What makes this dish special

This American Whole Corn Salad is a healthy and delicious snack that is loved by both kids and adults. It's a quick and easy recipe that can be made in a jiffy. The salad is packed with greens like cucumber, capsicum, and tomatoes, making it a nutritious option. Unlike other corn salads, this recipe doesn't use any oil or butter, making it a much healthier version. The combination of spices and lemon juice adds a burst of flavor to the salad. It can be served as is or in papad cones for a fun twist.

Recipe

Ingredients: * American whole corn: 1 cup (boiled and cooled) * Onion: 2 Tbsp (finely chopped) * Tomatoes: 2 Tbsp (finely chopped) * Cucumber: 2 Tbsp (finely chopped) * Capsicum: 2 Tbsp (finely chopped) * Green chili: 2 (finely chopped, optional) * Coriander: 2 Tbsp (finely chopped) * Salt: to taste * Black salt: ½ tsp * Chaat masala: ½ tsp * Kashmiri red chili powder: ½ tsp * Lemon juice: 1 Tbsp

Instructions: 1. In a bowl, mix all the ingredients well. 2. Serve chilled, garnished with coriander. 3. For a twist, serve in papad cones.

Architecture Tip

When building a house, it's important to plan for future needs. One common mistake is not considering whether to install window ACs or split ACs. If you initially opt for window ACs but later decide to switch to split ACs, dealing with the drain pipe and other pipes can be cumbersome. Therefore, while planning and constructing the house, make a provision for where you would place split ACs in the future. This way, you can leave the necessary punctures in the beam during casting, making the installation process much simpler when the time comes.
