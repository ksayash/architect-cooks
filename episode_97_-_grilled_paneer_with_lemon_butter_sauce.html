Grilled Paneer and Greens in Garlic Lemon Butter Sauce

What makes this dish special

This recipe is a quick and easy way to make a fancy and healthy dish. The grilled paneer and greens are cooked in a garlic lemon butter sauce, which adds a delicious flavor. The dish is visually appealing and tastes great. It's a healthy and flavorful meal that can be made in a short amount of time. The combination of grilled paneer, fresh greens, and a tangy lemon butter sauce creates a balanced and satisfying dish. The dish is perfect for a light lunch or a quick dinner. It's also a great way to get your daily dose of vegetables.

Recipe

Ingredients: * 150g paneer (cut into flat pieces) * ½ tsp mixed herbs * ½ tsp red chili flakes * 2 medium boiled potatoes (peeled and cut into halves) * ½ cup broccoli (cut into florets) * 1 carrot (cut into strips) * ½ cup beans (cut into 4" long pieces) * ½ cup cauliflower (cut into florets) * ½ cup mushrooms (cut into halves) * 2 tsp oil * 2 tsp butter * Salt to taste * 1 tsp pepper powder

For the sauce: * 4 Tbsp lemon juice * 1 Tbsp + 3 Tbsp butter * 1 Tbsp oil * 1 tsp minced garlic * 1 Tbsp finely chopped onion * 1 Tbsp all-purpose flour * ½ cup milk * 2-3 Tbsp fresh cream * ½ tsp pepper powder * 1 tsp finely chopped coriander/parsley * Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Steam or microwave all the vegetables (other than potatoes) so that they are slightly tender.
  2. In a pan, add 1 Tbsp butter and oil. Add the garlic. Saute for half a minute. Add the onion, saute till translucent. Add the flour and roast well. Add the milk while stirring continuously. Add cream and mix well. Add salt and pepper. Add the rest of the butter. Take off the flame and add the lemon juice and coriander. Mix well and keep aside. The sauce is ready.
  3. In a grill pan, add 1 tsp of oil and butter. Add the chili flakes and herbs. Grill the paneer pieces on both sides. Keep aside. In the same pan, grill the potatoes too.
  4. In another pan, add the remaining butter and oil. Add all the other vegetables. Saute with salt and pepper.
  5. Plate the paneer and vegetables. Pour the sauce and serve hot.

Tips: * When steaming or microwaving the vegetables, be careful not to overcook them. You want them to be slightly tender, but still have a bit of a crunch. * When making the sauce, be sure to stir constantly to prevent lumps from forming.
